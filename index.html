A holding company
built on conviction.

Zion River Holdings is a private family holding company based in Katy, Texas. It serves as the corporate parent of Doleman Enterprises LLC and the ventures that operate beneath it — each one built with a specific purpose, a specific audience, and a long view.

The companies span telecommunications, SaaS, creative publishing, music production, and Christian authorship. They are connected by a shared commitment to quality, integrity, and work that holds its value over time.
